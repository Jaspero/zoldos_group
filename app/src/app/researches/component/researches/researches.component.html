A Systems medicine approach to chronic inflammatory disease

Zoldoš group is involved in SYSCID project within the Workpackage WP9 (led by HUNIMED) by epigenetic reprogamming to cis-regulatory regions (i.e. pathogenic variants associated to CID, previously identified by partner Humanitas University, Milan, Italy) using dCas9 fusions (with activator and repressor domains, and/or epigenetic reader/eraser domains).

Author: Vlatka Zoldoš
